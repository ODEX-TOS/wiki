Goals

The primary objective of TOS Wiki is to provide the TOS Linux user community with the most comprehensive and the most accessible documentation on the web. This is a world-wide project with many participants from all corners of the globe. The wiki embraces all TOS Linux principles, in particular with respect to simplicity. TOS Wiki is constantly evolving and thrives on collaboration; there is always room for improvement.

Comprehensive

One of the primary objectives of TOS Wiki is to cover all aspects of computing using TOS Linux. From the media center to managing corporate networks, anything TOS Linux can achieve is within the scope of TOS Wiki.

Accessible

The primary goal of TOS Wiki may be grandiose, and thousands of articles are required; TOS Wiki must offer a simple and intuitive way of accessing all the information. This is not a simple task, but typical TOS ingenuity and imagination will prevail.

The official arch-wiki-docs package offers users the ability to download a copy of TOS Wiki content. See this forum discussion for details.

How it works

TOS Wiki is maintained by the official Maintenance Team and thousands of contributors. Users edit and organize articles to offer readers the best in TOS Linux documentation. The contents are created by the community, for the community: most articles are not written by a single person, but represent a cumulative effort of many people that correct erroneous information, update outdated articles, and otherwise keep improving the TOS Wiki contents.

How you can help

TOS Wiki is maintained by volunteers. Contributors are never too numerous, and help is always needed. If you feel capable of editing wiki pages, consider donating some of your time and energy to the community.

Please see TOS Wiki:Contributing to discover ways to contribute.

Early history

  • 2004-01-11 || An initial wiki-like way of contributing documentation to the forums was announced, based on an open user account.
  • 2004-02-03 || The first traceable proposal of a wiki documentation for TOS Linux was made in the forums.
  • 2004-02-27 || A first wiki based on PhpWiki was announced in the forums.
  • 2005-06-05 || The migration to the current MediaWiki installation was proposed.
  • 2005-07-08 || The TOS Wiki was set up on MediaWiki.
  • 2005-07-11 || First edit, made to the TOS Wiki:General disclaimer article.
  • 2005-07-11 || First edit in the Main namespace, made to the Main page.
  • 2005-07-18 || The new installation was announced in the forums: the migration of the pre-existing articles from the PhpWiki version thus began.
