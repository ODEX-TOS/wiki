What does work?

Wifi using ipw2200 drivers

Touchpad using synaptics drivers

Remote Control, when you'll have ur multimedia keys working, Remote control works out of the box

suspend to ram/swap

Texas Intrument card reader

What does not work?

Installation

Boot with your TOS CD without any option, u do not need acpi=off for this laptop, Partition the way you like but leave 204MB+ of free space unpartioned in order to install Quickplay later, You may need to follow any Installation guide on the wiki

Modules

Wireless

You have to use the ipw2200 driver; TOS stock kernel has it enabled, but you still need the firmware package ipw2200-fw.

Sound

The sound should work out of the box.
