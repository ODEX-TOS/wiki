Configuration

This is based on the 2012 HP Envy 17.

Video

Works by following http://www.reddit.com/r/linux/comments/1nu4pc/muxless_graphics_cards_on_linux/. It is recommended to switch to linux-mainlineAUR to take advantage of the power-management features built into 3.12.

Audio

hdajackretask in the package alsa-tools can be used to remap the unconnected 0x10 pin to the subwoofer (Internal Speaker LFE).

SD Card Reader

As of 3.8 a third party module is not needed, device is accessible /dev/mmcX.
